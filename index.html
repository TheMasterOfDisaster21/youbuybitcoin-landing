The Story

YouBuyBitcoin.com was registered in October 2017. Bitcoin was around $5,000. Then came the crash. 80% down. Everyone said crypto was dead.

The domain was never sold. Never parked. Never squatted on. It was held through the bear market, through the doubt, through the years.

I could have slapped an affiliate link on it in 2018 and made money. But I didn't want to be another crypto content mill. I wanted to build something that would outlast me.

This is mission work. A permanent, free, open resource for anyone who wants to understand and use Bitcoin. If it works, it works for 50 years. If it doesn't, free Bitcoin education still exists forever.

— Marius, The Master Of Disaster

October 2017 → March 2026 — 8 years of conviction.
